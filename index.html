About Us

🩷 Sheltr is a citizen-led, volunteer-powered platform that connects people displaced by emergencies with safe, temporary places to stay — free of cost and free of judgment. Born from the urgent need to respond quickly in times of crisis, Sheltr was created to bridge compassion with action. Whether it's floods, fires, or other disasters, we believe that help should be fast, accessible, and human.
